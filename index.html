NODE / 01 ENTERPRISE / BPM

Process design that doesn't break.

Agents earned, not assembled.

Two harnesses I've built and lived with: chorus-agent on the SS&C AI Gateway, kindle-pipeline on local Ollama. Same twelve-component vocabulary, opposite axiom sets. The interesting design is always in the constraints, not the checklist.
