About Me

Hi, I'm Chris Moore, a process implementation consultant based in Kansas City. I work with teams to simplify the way business gets done. My focus is helping organizations streamline operations using SS&C Blue Prism Chorus so work moves efficiently and people can spend more time on what matters.


What keeps me motivated is the puzzle of it all. I love digging into complexity, cutting through the noise, and building practical systems that solve real problems.
